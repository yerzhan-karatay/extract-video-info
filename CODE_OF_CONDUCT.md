# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to keep this project respectful, practical, and focused on useful software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent when feedback is ambiguous.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the project.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, spam, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who repeatedly disrupt the project.
